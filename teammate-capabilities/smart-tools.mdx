## Smart Tools

- **Airtable**: Store and query data in Airtable.
- **Curate**: Query and download news articles from Curate.
- **Fireflies**: Download transcripts and summaries from meetings and calls
- **G Mail**: Send and receive emails.
- **GitHub**: Track changes to code.
- **Google Drive**: Read and write files and folders on Google Drive.
- **Google Sheets**: Read and write data to and from Google Sheets
- **Google Slides**: Create and modify Google Slides presentations.
- **Hubspot**: Manage customer relationships.
- **Meta Ads**: Manage ads on Meta's platforms including Facebook, Instagram, and Messenger.
- **Notion**: Create and manage pages and databases in Notion.
- **Printify**: Create and manage custom printed products.
- **Slack**: Send and receive chat messages in real-time.
- **Zapier**: Trigger preconfigured web-based automations.
